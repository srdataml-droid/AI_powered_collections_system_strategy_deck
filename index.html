🤖 How it works

Trained on real Geldium customer data using a Random Forest model. Scores customers across 8 financial features to predict delinquency risk. Each prediction includes an AI-generated explanation powered by Gemini.

🛡️ Responsible AI

Fairness monitoring across customer segments. Explainable predictions in plain language. Human oversight for all High-risk escalations. ECOA and GDPR aligned design.

Risk Tier Guide

🔴 HIGH RISK
Credit utilization >80% + missed payments → Escalate to human agent immediately
🟡 MEDIUM RISK
Elevated utilization or debt burden → Proactive outreach + repayment plan offer
🟢 LOW RISK
Stable repayment behaviour → Automated payment reminder
